Frequent Questions

How can I find books in a specific genre on your website?

You can browse books by genre using our genre filter located on the main menu or by visiting the 'Genres' section. You can also use the search bar to find specific books or authors.

Do you offer book recommendations?

Yes, we provide personalized book recommendations based on your reading history and preferences. You can also check out our curated lists and featured recommendations on the homepage.

How can I leave a review for a book?

To leave a review, simply navigate to the book's page and click on the 'Write a Review' button. You can rate the book, share your thoughts, and help other readers make informed choices.

Do you have a book club or discussion forums?

Yes, we have an online book club and discussion forums where members can discuss books, share insights, and connect with fellow readers. You can join a group that matches your interests or start your own.

How can I track my reading progress?

You can track your reading progress by logging into your account and using our reading tracker feature. It allows you to mark books as 'Currently Reading,' 'Want to Read,' or 'Read,' and provides stats on your reading habits.
